Turret Game

  • WASD - Move player (cyan circle)
  • Green Enemy - Prey (flees from you)
  • Red Enemy - Hunter (chases you)
  • Gold Coins - Resources (collect them)
  • Black Squares - Turrets (auto-shoot enemies)
  • Mouse Click - Place turret (1 per wave from wave 2+)

Goal: Survive waves, collect resources, place turrets strategically!
